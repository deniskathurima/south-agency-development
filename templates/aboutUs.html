ABOUT US

The idea to set up the organization was initiated by Sheikh. Mabrouk Aguer Thiep Aguer Incollaboration with the other directors. The two stakeholders collaborated to form the organization which was registered on 151 May 2043 by the Relief and Rehabilitation Commission. The R.R.C is the official body in charge of registration of Non-Profit Organizations in South Sudan.

The main primary goal being; providing sustainable development initiative to empower the community and eradicate political motivated ethnic violence in South Sudan Region specifically the Bahr El Ghazal Region; Aweil, Wau, Kwajok,Warrap, Rumbek, Abyei Region. The organization has managed to open thirty eight branches in South Sudan which are managed by seven officials in the executive senior management level and sixteen officials are the administration management level.
